Ceviche

Ceviche is a popular Peruvian dish made with fresh raw fish or seafood marinated in citrus juices, usually lime or lemon. The acidity of the citrus juice "cooks" the fish, giving it a firm texture and a tangy flavor. It is typically seasoned with onions, hot peppers, and cilantro, which add a refreshing kick to the dish.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb white fish fillets, cubed
  • 1 cup lime juice
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 hot pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the fish, lime juice, and salt. Let it marinate for 30 minutes.
  2. Add the sliced onion, hot pepper, and cilantro. Mix well.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving.
